About Me

My research area is Machine Learning for Software Engineering, supervised by Dr. Leandro Minku and Dr. Rami Bahsoon. My PhD is fully funded by University of Birmingham.

I have an MSc in Advanced Software Engineering from University of Leicester. After my undergraduate in Computer Science and Engineering, I have worked as a frontend developer in a multi national digital agency in London.

My Work

During my PhD, I worked in the project SPDISC (Stable Prediction of Defect-Inducing Software Changes). This project aims at creating more stable models for predicting defect-inducing changes, through the development of a novel machine learning approach for automatically adapting to concept drift.

I have developed novel approaches for online cross-project JIT-SDP. My work on this area has been accepted in ICSE 2020

For my MSc thesis, I developed an approach for solving touristic route planning problem using Evolutionary algorithm. I have designed an efficient fitness function for the algorithm which leads to very good results to solve the problem.

Previously as a Frontend developer in an Agile Scrum team, I have worked in different projects including development of different data driven websites.
Besides, I also have experience in scientific journal reviewing.
